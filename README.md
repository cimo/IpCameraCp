IpCamera cp
==============

Framework for management your IpCamera.

Based on "Uebusaito" project.
You can record video and take image, setting your IpCamera and use a motion detection.
WORK IN PROGRESS...

| Cross platform (Windows, Linux, Mac, Android, Ios) |
